seawater.k¶

Release:1.1
Date:Nov 08, 2017
seawater.k(T, S, P)[source][source]¶

Compute the thermal conductivity of seawater

Evaluates the thermal conductivity of seawater as a function of temperature, pressure, and salinity following equations in Sharqawy et al. (2010), Table 4.

Parameters:

T : float

temperature (K)

S : float

salinity (psu)

P : float

pressure (Pa)
Returns:

k : float

thermal conductivity of seawater (W/(mK))

Notes

Table 4 provides three equations. Equation (14) is valid for temperatures up to 60 deg C, but I could not reproduce the values in the paper. Hence, the slightly less-accurate equation (13) is used for temperatures above 30 deg C.

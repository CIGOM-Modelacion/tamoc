seawater.sigma¶

Release:0.1
Date:September 11, 2013
seawater.sigma(T)[source]¶

Compute the surface tension of seawater

Evaluates the surface tension of seawater as a function of temperature per data in the CRC handbook and fit following methods in Leifer et al. (2000). Note that Leifer et al. (2000) do not present a correlation for sigma; rather, this function just follows their methodology for viscosity.

Parameters :

T : float

temperature (K)
Returns :

sigma : float

interfacial tension of air in seawater (N/m)

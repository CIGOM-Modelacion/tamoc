dbm.InsolubleParticle.mass_by_diameter¶

Release:0.1
Date:February 26, 2014
InsolubleParticle.mass_by_diameter(de, T, P, Sa, Ta)[source]¶

Compute the mass (kg) of an inert fluid particle with equivalent spherical diameter de.

Parameters :

de : float

equivalent spherical diameter (m)

T : float

particle temperature (K)

P : float

particle pressure (Pa)

Sa : float

salinity of ambient seawater (psu)

Ta : float

temperature of ambient seawater (K)
Returns :

m : float

mass of the fluid particle (kg)

seawater.sigma¶

seawater.sigma(T, S)[source]¶

Compute the surface tension of seawater

Evaluates the surface tension of seawater as a function of temperature and salinity following equations in Sharqawy et al. (2010), Table 6.

Parameters
Tfloat

temperature (K)

Sfloat

salinity (psu)

Returns
sigmafloat

interfacial tension of air in seawater (N/m)

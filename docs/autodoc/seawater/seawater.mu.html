seawater.mu¶

seawater.mu(T, S, P)[source]¶

Compute the viscosity of seawater

Evaluates the viscosity of seawater as a function of temperature, salinity, and pressure. The equation accounting for temperature and salinity is from Sharqawy et al. (2010). The pressure correction is from McCain (1990), equation B-75 on page 528.

Parameters
Tfloat

temperature (K)

Sfloat

salinity (psu)

Pfloat

pressure (Pa)

Returns
mufloat

dynamic viscosity of seawater (Pa s)

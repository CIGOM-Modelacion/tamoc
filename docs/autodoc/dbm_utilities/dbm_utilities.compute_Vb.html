dbm_utilities.compute_Vb¶

dbm_utilities.compute_Vb(Vc)[source]¶

Compute the molar volume at the boiling point

Compute the specific molar volume at the boiling point from the specific molar volume at the critical point using the Tyn and Calus formula (see dbm.py for details).

Parameters
Vcfloat or np.array

Specific molar volume at the critical point (m^3/mol)

Returns
Vbfloat or np.array

Specific molar volume at the boiling point (m^3/mol)

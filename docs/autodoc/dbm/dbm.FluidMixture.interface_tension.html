dbm.FluidMixture.interface_tension¶

FluidMixture.interface_tension(self, m, T, S, P)[source]¶

Computes the interfacial tension between gas/liquid and water

If air is False (thus, assume hydrocarbon), this method computes the interfacial tension between the gas and liquid phases of the mixture and water using equations in Danesh (1998) “PVT and Phase Behaviour Of Petroleum Reservoir Fluids,” Chapter 8. Otherwise, we treat the fluid like air and use the surface tension of seawater from Sharqawy et al. (2010), Table 6.

Parameters
mndarray, size (nc)

masses of each component in a mixture (kg)

Tfloat

mixture temperature (K)

Pfloat

mixture pressure (Pa)

Returns
sigma_pndarray, size (2)

interfacial tension for gas (row 1) and liquid (row 2) (N/m)

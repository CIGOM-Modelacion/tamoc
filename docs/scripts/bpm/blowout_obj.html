Blowout Module: Blowout Simulation¶

This script demonstrates using the blowout.Blowout class to simulate a synthetic subsea accidental oil well blowout. This class does much of the work that would otherwise be done through scripting interfaces to the TAMOC suite of modules and functions.

Here, we simulate a synthetic blowout with a dead oil flow rate of 20000 bbl/d and a gas-to-oil ratio of 2000 released through a 0.2 m diameter orifice at 1000 m depth. We use the dbm.FluidMixture object to compute the oil and gas equilibrium and properties, and we use the particle_size_models to compute the gas bubble and oil droplet size distributions.
